| ABOUT TATTOOS |

The Artistry behinf tattoos can be seen as the intimate and special bond shared between client and creator, working together to achieve a design that is persoanl to the receiver but also contriute to the creative journey of the artist. It shoud be seen as a shared experience opposed to just a service. Devotion is another key aspect to tattoos for all parties involved. In order to have something essentially branded on your body, you must be devoted to that design, and to what it means. And it is just as important for the artist to be devoted to givign their client the best possible outcome. Lastly, Passion is often a forgotten element of tattoing, with tattoo culture being so widely spread in modern society. However it is passion that motivates the process at its core, from the design to the baring of pain.
